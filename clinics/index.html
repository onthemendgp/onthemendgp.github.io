Working with practices

Health education for more waiting rooms

On The Mend works with GP practices and local health services to share short, plain-English health videos, in waiting rooms and on clinic websites. The aim is simple: help more people understand their health.

For patients

What this means for you

You might come across On The Mend playing in your clinic's waiting room, or as a link on your practice's website. It is free health education: short conversations with GPs, nurses and health workers about everyday topics.

It shares general information only. It is not personalised medical advice, and it never replaces a conversation with your own GP.

The fine print

Compliance, in plain terms

On The Mend content is produced with AHPRA advertising guidelines and TGA requirements in mind. Episodes provide general information only, carry clear disclaimers, avoid testimonials about clinical outcomes, and make no therapeutic claims. Guest credits are simple statements of fact about where a practitioner works.

Nothing on On The Mend replaces advice from a patient's own GP.
