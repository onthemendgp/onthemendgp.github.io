Setup

How it works

We create your clinic's view. Your practice name, your featured doctors, and your local exclusions, configured by us.

We send you one line of code. A small On The Mend button, styled to match your site. WordPress and Squarespace ready. Your web person pastes it in.

Done. Clicks open your patients' view in a new tab. No accounts, no logins, no ongoing admin, no cost.

Example button code:

<a href="https://onthemendgp.com.au/episodes/?p=yourclinic" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> Patient health videos: On The Mend </a>
The fine print

Compliance, in plain terms

On The Mend content is produced with AHPRA advertising guidelines and TGA requirements in mind. Episodes provide general information only, carry clear disclaimers, avoid testimonials about clinical outcomes, and make no therapeutic claims. Guest credits are simple statements of fact about where a practitioner works.

The button links to educational content, not promotional material, and the resource is free for partner clinics. Nothing on On The Mend replaces advice from a patient's own GP.
