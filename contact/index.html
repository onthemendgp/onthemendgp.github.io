For guests

What happens after you reach out

We reply and have a chat. A quick conversation about your topic, what you want to cover, and what to expect on the day.

You receive a guest welcome pack. It explains the format, and includes a consent form so you know exactly how the recording will be used.

We record at the studio. A relaxed conversation, usually done within an hour. No preparation beyond knowing your own work is needed.

You approve before anything goes live. Nothing is published without your sign-off, and all content is checked against Australian health content guidelines.
